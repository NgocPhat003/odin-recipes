Fried rice recipes

What the dish will look like after finished

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Cooking oil
  • Carrot
  • Pea
  • Chinese sausage
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Put oil into the pan
  2. Put the egg in and scramble it
  3. Add the rice and mix the egg and rice together
  4. Chop off carrot, pea and chinese sausage
  5. Put them into the pan and mix the all up for about 5 minutes
